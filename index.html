Admission, discharge & transport

2025 WHO

Level III NICU admission: BW <1500g, GA <34w, severe sepsis/shock, CPAP/ventilation, major surgery. Family integrated care â€“ parents 24/7 presence.

Neonatal transport standards 2025

Transport incubator (servo-controlled), pre-warmed, emergency meds, trained team. Embrace/transwarmer for low-resource. Maternal escort whenever possible.

Neonatal resuscitation (NRP 8th/ILCOR 2024)

  • DCC: delayed cord clamping â‰¥60s (VLBW 60s with cord intact).
  • O2: term 21%, preterm <32w 21-30%, target SpO2 nomogram.
  • Non-vigorous meconium: NO routine tracheal suction â€“ CPAP/PPV if apnoeic.
  • Preterm <32w: plastic wrap, exothermic mattress, T-piece, CPAP 6-8 cmH2O.
  • Epinephrine IV/IO 0.02 mg/kg (0.2ml/kg of 1:10,000) if HR <60.

Respiratory support & LISA

EBNEO 2025

RDS & surfactant

Eur RDS 2025

Antenatal steroids up to 34+6w. Rescue surfactant (poractant alfa 200 mg/kg) if FiO2 >0.30 on CPAP. Repeat if FiO2 >0.40, MAP >8.

Patent ductus arteriosus

2024 PDA-TOLERATE

First-line IV paracetamol 15 mg/kg q6h x3d (less nephrotoxicity, equal efficacy). Ibuprofen alternative. Ligation if medical failure + ventilator dependent.

PPHN

Neonatal network 2024

Target SpO2 95-100%, iNO if OI >20, sildenafil 0.5-2 mg/kg q6h, milrinone for low output. Avoid hypocarbia.

Neonatal sepsis / meningitis

NeoOBS 2025

EOS: ampicillin + gentamicin. LONS: vancomycin + cefotaxime. Fluconazole prophylaxis ELBW. Duration: culture-negative 5-7d, culture-positive 14d, meningitis 21d.

Neonatal tuberculosis

WHO 2024

Congenital TB: hepatosplenomegaly, respiratory distress, gastric aspirate Xpert. INH 10 mg/kg + RMP 15 mg/kg + PZA 35 mg/kg + EMB 20 mg/kg x2m, then INH+RMP 4-7m.

HIV exposed infant

2024 WHO

NVP or AZT for 6-12w, PCR at birth, 6w, 4m. Co-trimoxazole from 4-6w. Breastfeeding encouraged if mother on ART.

Congenital CMV, syphilis

CDC 2025

cCMV: SNHL, petechiae; valganciclovir 6w. Syphilis: crystalline penicillin 10-14d.

Nutrition & TPN

ESPGHAN 2025

AA 2.5-3.5 g/kg/d day1; lipids 2-3 g/kg/d. Trophic 10-20 ml/kg/d D1. HMF at 100 ml/kg/d. Vitamin D 800 IU/d VLBW.

Inborn errors of metabolism

SIMD 2024

Suspected: encephalopathy, metabolic acidosis, hypoglycemia, hyperammonemia. Emergency: stop protein, IV glucose, sodium benzoate, carnitine.

Neonatal jaundice

AAP 2024

Hour-specific phototherapy, IVIG for isoimmune hemolysis, exchange transfusion nomogram. B/A ratio guide.

Acute kidney injury / hydronephrosis

KDIGO stage 1: sCr rise â‰¥0.3 mg/dL. Avoid nephrotoxins. Postnatal US for ANH day 3-7, amoxicillin prophylaxis if suspected PUV.

Anemia & transfusion

NICE 2025

PRBC 15 ml/kg. Thresholds: ventilated day1-7 Hb <12 g/dL, stable >2w Hb <8.5 g/dL. Hold feeds 1h before/after transfusion (NEC reduction).

Intraventricular haemorrhage prevention

Antenatal steroids, DCC, minimal handling, caffeine. Cranial US for <32w: day5-7 & day28.

ROP & hearing screening

ICROP3 2024

Screen: BW <1501g or GA <32w, plus unstable 1500-2000g. Type1 ROP: laser/anti-VEGF. Hearing: AABR/OAE before discharge.

Surgical emergencies

CDH: avoid mask, immediate intubation, NG, iNO. Gastroschisis: bowel bag, right side, sutureless closure. MMC closure <48h.

Necrotizing enterocolitis

Stage I: NPO, Abx 3d. Stage II/III: NPO, TPN, Abx (amp+gent+metro), surgery if pneumoperitoneum.

Pain & developmental care

PIPP-R 2025

Sucrose 24% 0.1-1 ml 2min pre-procedure; facilitated tucking, KMC, EMLA. Noise <45dB, cycled lighting.

Developmental care

Cue-based care, parental engagement, non-nutritive sucking, sleep protection.

Ethics, palliative care & LAMA

2024 WHO

Palliative: symptom control (morphine), family support. LAMA: empathic counselling, explore reasons, social work, document.

Maternal mental health in NICU

2025 AAP

Screen for PPD/ anxiety (EPDS), psychological first aid, peer support, involve mother in cares.

Trisomies (DS, T18, T13)

Down syndrome: cardiac echo, thyroid screen. T18/T13: often palliative.

Thyroid dysfunction

ESPE 2024

Immunization (EPI)

Preterm: chronological age, full dose. HepB birth dose + HBIG if mother HBsAg+.
